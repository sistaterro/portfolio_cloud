About Me

Background

My expertise as a language tutor aligns well with the qualifications you desire. I hold a Master of Arts degree in Media and Culture and a TEFL certificate. My passion lies in educating English and Dutch online to adults, all around the world. Furthermore, I have been educating English for five years at "Longwell Education Center" in China.

Managing the speaking abilities of various students often proves to be challenging, though gratifying. I tailor activities to maintain the interest, critical, didactic correctness. Thanks to my experience, I learned how to incorporate practical concepts that are playful and easy to grasp.
